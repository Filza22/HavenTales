Romance

A romance story is a type of narrative that focuses on the emotional and romantic relationship between two (or more) people.

The Notebook

A heartwarming story of Noah and Allie, two young lovers from different social classes. Despite being separated by circumstances and time, their love survives years. A timeless tale of devotion, memory, and unconditional love.

Pride and Prejudice

Elizabeth Bennet and Mr. Darcy initially clash due to pride and misunderstandings. As they learn more about each other, love blossoms. The story explores themes of class, character, and the power of love to overcome judgment.

Me Before You

Louisa, a cheerful girl, becomes a caregiver for Will, a paralyzed man. Their bond grows deep, but Will's pain and choices challenge their relationship. A moving tale about love, loss, and living life fully

I Too Had a Love Story

A true love story of Ravin and Khushi, who meet online and fall deeply in love. Just when everything seems perfect, tragedy strikes. A heartfelt, emotional story of love and heartbreak

P.S. I Love You

After Gerry's death, his wife Holly receives letters he wrote before he died—each one helping her heal and move forward. A touching story of love that lives on even after death.

It Ends With Us

Lily falls for Ryle, but his dark side and her past resurface. Caught between love and pain, she must make tough choices. A powerful story of self-worth, survival, and breaking cycles.
