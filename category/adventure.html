ADVENTURE

An adventure story is a thrilling journey filled with danger, discovery, and daring feats.

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

The first book in the Harry Potter series, where a young boy discovers he is a wizard and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

The Lightning Thief

Percy Jackson discovers he's a demigod, the son of Poseidon, and must retrieve Zeus`s stolen lightning bolt to prevent a war among the gods. A fast-paced mix of modern adventure and Greek mythology

Hatchet

Thirteen-year-old Brian survives a plane crash and must live alone in the Canadian wilderness with only a hatchet. A gripping survival story of resilience, nature, and self-discovery.

The Hobbit

Bilbo, a simple hobbit, joins a group of dwarves on a treasure hunt. He faces dragons, trolls, and his own courage.

Around the World in Eighty Days

English gentleman Phileas Fogg bets he can circle the globe in 80 days. He embarks on a whirlwind journey with his servant, encountering danger, excitement, and love along the way.

The Call of the Wild

Buck, a domestic dog, is stolen and sold into the brutal life of an Alaskan sled dog. He must adapt to the wild and eventually embraces his primal instincts in this powerful tale of survival and nature.
