Kids

A kids' story is a short, imaginative, and moral-based tale written especially for children.

The Tortoise and the Hare

A slow tortoise races a fast hare. The hare is overconfident and takes a nap, while the tortoise slowly keeps moving and wins the race.

The Ugly Duckling

A little duckling is teased for looking different, but grows into a beautiful swan. Moral: Don't judge others by their appearance; everyone has inner beauty.

Cinderella

A kind girl is treated badly by her stepmother and stepsisters but finds happiness with the help of a fairy godmother.

The Cat in the Hat

A magical cat comes to play with two kids on a rainy day and turns their house into a mess—but fixes it before their mother returns.

The Tale of Peter Rabbit

A naughty rabbit enters a garden against his mother’s warning and faces danger but learns a lesson.

The Jungle Book

A boy named Mowgli is raised in the jungle by animals and learns important lessons about friendship, courage, and nature.
